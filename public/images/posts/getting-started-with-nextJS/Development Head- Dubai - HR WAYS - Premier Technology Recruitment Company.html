Development Head- Dubai

Job Category: Development Head
Job Type: Full Time
Job Location: Dubai
Facebook Twitter LinkedIn WhatsApp

Hiring Development Head for a Leading Property E-Commerce Store established in 1986, They currently has offices and franchises across the region, connecting people locally and globally with its 400+ plus team of certified consultants and staff, supplemented by an extensive property database, magazine and interactive website. Offering easier ways to discover lucrative real estate opportunities, the company has grown to become one of the Region’s most recognised and respected brands, specialising in residential and commercial property, property management, short-term rentals, and off-plan.

Job Responsibilities

  • Define the right web architecture to support the needs of our different businesses in the real estate industry and various B2B and B2C services.
  • Build an outstanding tenant/ landlord app that will become the most widely used real estate customer journey app in the UAE and will innovate the currently antiquated customer experience within real estate brokerage.
  • Build an outstanding mortgage journey app similar to that of huspy.com and will become the most widely used mortgage app in the UAE.
  • Lead the development of an up to date and scalable solution for our multiple legacy websites.
  • Lead the migration of the old websites to the new unified platform.
  • Establish good coding practices, documenting, and apply agile methodologies to maximize the performance of the tech team.
  • Lead the adoption of an up to date web development environment with code versioning, code backup, staging server, deployment system, automations, and so on.
  • Lead the development of our mobile apps, especially from the backend and database side.
  • Stay up to date with the latest trends in web and mobile app development.

Other Details:

  • Salary: Upto 22000 AED
  • Experience: 10 to 15 Years
  • Location: UAE
